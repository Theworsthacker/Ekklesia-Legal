# Privacy Policy
Last Updated: February 14, 2026

Ekklesia ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Ekklesia mobile application (the "App").

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Information You Provide
We may collect information that you voluntarily provide, including:

- Name or username
- Email address
- Profile information
- Church affiliation (if provided)
- Posts, comments, and other content you upload
- Subscription or payment details (processed via Apple)

### B. Automatically Collected Information
When you use the App, we may automatically collect:

- Device type
- Operating system
- App usage data
- IP address
- Crash and diagnostics data

### C. Location Data
If you enable location services, we may collect approximate location information to display nearby churches and events.

You may disable location permissions at any time through your device settings.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

- Create and manage user accounts
- Display nearby churches and community events
- Enable posting and community interaction
- Improve and maintain the App
- Prevent abuse and maintain safety
- Comply with legal obligations

---

## 3. Age Requirement

Ekklesia is rated for users ages 9 and older.

Users under the age of 13 should use the App only with parental or guardian supervision.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

If we become aware that such information has been collected, we will take steps to delete it.

---

We use trusted third-party services, including:

- Firebase (Google LLC) for authentication, database, and analytics
- Apple for subscription and payment processing

These providers may collect information in accordance with their own privacy policies:

Firebase: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy  
Apple: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

Content you post (including comments, discussions, and event participation) may be visible to other users.

You are responsible for the information you choose to share publicly.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to:
